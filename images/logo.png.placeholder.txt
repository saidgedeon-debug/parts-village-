=== REPLACE THIS FILE WITH YOUR LOGO ===

To add your logo:
1. Delete this file
2. Copy your logo image here
3. Rename it to "logo.png" (or logo.jpg)
4. The logo will automatically appear on:
   - Quotations
   - Invoices
   - Receipts
   - Supplier Inquiries

Recommended logo size: 200x80 pixels (transparent PNG preferred)
